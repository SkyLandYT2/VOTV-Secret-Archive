Ariral Rep Events

Description:

While there are storymode events that occur on scripted/triggerable timelines, there is an Ariral Rep system or Ariral Reputation system which boils down to your relationship with your Ariral neighbors at their treehouse.

There are a total of 5 tiers with -100 to 100 as the range. Each tier encapsulates 50 rep points. The tiers from lowest to highest being: mean, inconvenient, normal, good, and loyal. You can either gain or lose points depending on certain actions. For an example, giving shrimp the Arirals will give you 20 points. To lose points, actions will include: setting explosions at the treehouse, stealing items, erasing Ariral graffiti drawings, and microwaving or damaging an Argemia Plush.

Throughout storymode you will encounter certain events that can only be triggered by certain rep tiers or points. Technically speaking there are 5 stories or playthroughs that you can perform in storymode. These events while rep based, could occur on only certain days.
