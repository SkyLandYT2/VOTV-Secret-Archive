Planet Signals

Description:

A lot of the signals in VOTV are signals of real life planets, exoplanets, and objects that can be found in space and have been observed by us. These signals are actual live audio sounds of said objects. While some of these signals are references to some medias.

For each signal audio sound there will be a little button click or notification, that is to signify the start of the signal audio.
