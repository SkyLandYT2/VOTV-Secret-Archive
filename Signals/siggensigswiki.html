SigGens Signals

Description:

When the the 0.6.0 update was released, VOTV recieved 40+ new signals with the two main types being SigGen6 and SigGen602 signals. Its unknown why these specific signals are named that way but it is how it is in the datatables.

For each signal audio sound there will be a little button click or notification, that is to signify the start of the signal audio.
